Our Story

A Krishna Nagar landmark, built on trust.

Founded with a single chair and a big dream, Pink Scissor has grown into Mathura's most loved unisex salon & academy. What began as a passion for the craft of hair and beauty is now a destination trusted by over a thousand families across the city.

Every artist at Pink Scissor trains under senior educators before ever picking up a client's scissor — because for us, beauty is a discipline, not a shortcut. From everyday grooming to once-in-a-lifetime bridal transformations, we bring international technique to the heart of Mathura.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Where is Pink Scissor Salon located?

+

We're located in Krishna Nagar, Mathura, Uttar Pradesh — easily accessible with parking available nearby. See the map below for exact directions.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

+

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking in advance — especially for bridal and party packages — to guarantee your preferred artist and time slot.

What are the academy admission requirements?

+

No prior experience is required for our foundation courses. We welcome all beginners and provide certification upon successful completion.

Do you offer bridal trial sessions?

+

Yes, every bridal package includes at least one trial session so you can finalise your look before the big day, stress-free.

What safety and hygiene measures do you follow?

+

All tools are sanitised between clients, we use single-use consumables where applicable, and our studio undergoes daily deep cleaning.

What are your operating hours?

+

We're open all days of the week from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, including weekends and most public holidays.
